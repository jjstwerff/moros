# Master Darius

- **Role:** Forge-master of a factory district in World Edge
- **Species:** Badger (animal-person)
- **Location:** World Edge
- **Status:** Alive

## Summary

A badger who runs a forge district in World Edge, carrying the smell of forge-work so deeply into his skin that it seems part of him. He is fair-minded and values honest labour above convenient prejudice: **Remi**, a rat, did the jobs nobody else would — crawling into dark spaces, reading machinery by touch, working hours that never appeared on official records — and Darius saw the worth of it. When the mayor ordered all rat-kind expelled from World Edge, Darius came to the factory door to say goodbye and handed Remi a letter of recommendation, quietly refusing to pretend the expulsion measured anything about Remi personally.

## Relationships

- Employer and informal mentor to **Remi** (the rat expelled from World Edge — distinct from Hank's partner Egbert at Elmsfield).
- Stands in contrast to (and quietly opposes) the Mayor of World Edge's expulsion order.
- **Queen Fienna** of Allondo is from the **Leatherman house** of World's End — an outwards-facing industrial family. The Leathermans are pursuing a **railroad project** aimed first at Raft city (commercial connection across or around the forbidden woods) and eventually south to Fata morgana and Gap city. Any such project runs through Darius's forge district by necessity (rails, rolling stock, engine components). Whether Darius and Fienna have spoken personally is open, but his forges are one of the project's material spines. If the railroad ever gets built, some of its iron will have passed through his hands.
