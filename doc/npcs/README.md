# NPCs

One file per named NPC in the Moros setting. See [goals.md](goals.md) for what every NPC is meant to contribute to a session.

Related: [cards.md](cards.md) — player-facing NPC cards (earned by the party through play) and [DM_STAGING.md](../claude/DM_STAGING.md) — DM briefing for session-by-session NPC introduction order and reveal layers.

## Ancient powers still active

- [Laurent](laurent.md) — demi-lich in the wreck of his own crashed flying city; wants it revived and wants revenge.
- [Felicia](felicia.md) — great spirit enshrined at Luchebert Monastery; settled the post-crash demi-humans from beyond her own death.

## Historical / Legendary

- [Narissa](narissa.md) — the First Mage; diary sparked the Age of Wild Magic.
- [Thorgal](thorgal.md) — overseas mage who led the Overseas War out of his continent's starvation; killed by John Bean (through his own scrying mirror).

## Overseas

- [Raul](raul.md) — ox-tauren war-band leader; captured Hannes; retreated when Thorgal's mirror broke. Alive, abroad.

## Royal house of Allondo

- [King Hannes](king_hannes.md) — king of Allondo; holds John Bean's enlistment right to Ron.
- [Queen Fienna](queen_fienna.md) — Hannes's wife and co-ruler.
- [Prince Corven](prince_corven.md) — Crown Prince of Allondo; martial heir; secretly in love with a Brumal princess.
- [Didrich](didrich.md) — second son of Hannes; young; target of Irna's non-lethal attack.

## Royal house of Brumal

- [Princess Penelope](princess_penelope.md) — Brumal royal lady; secretly in love with Prince Corven.

## Bean family and their circle

- [John Bean](john_bean.md) — Boar folk (Tusk Clan) war hero; telekinesis through an ironwood arm; accidentally ended the Overseas War.
- [Irna Bean](irna_bean.md) — John's daughter; gravely ill in Elmsfield after fungus exposure at the Lake of Tears.
- [Ron Bean](ron_bean.md) — John's younger son; subject of Father Elliot's disputed "valiant spirit" proclamation.
- [Lady Rhianna Linthrope](rhianna_linthrope.md) — Mouse-clan priestess/crafter in Allondo; fitted John's arm in the war; got Irna out of mining.
- [Felix](felix.md) — raccoon scout; John's wartime companion.
- [Father Elliot](father_elliot.md) — human abbot of Luchebert Monastery; former Crown Prince of Allondo; source of the proclamation; shelters those who need to disappear.
- [Willow](willow.md) — old rabbit-folk granny of Luchebert; tends Felicia's shrine; keeper of the Abbey's quiet truth.

## Elmsfield locals

- [Elder Diederik](elder_diederik.md) — Owl elder of Elmsfield; exiled druid; knows ironwood; keeps Irna alive.
- [Innkeeper Bridget](bridget.md) — Badger; runs the Inn inside Elmsfield's great ironwood tree; wants to expand.
- [Wilfred](wilfred.md) — Badger farmer; Bridget's husband.
- [Farmer Joseph](farmer_joseph.md) — Raccoon post-war refugee; bought a plot from Hank; slinger; carries post-war cholera trauma; Session 1's opening obstacle.
- [Hank (Hanny)](hank.md) — Rabbit folk farmer presenting as male; runs an orphanage of sorts on the edge of Elmsfield; lead out into the world.
- [Egbert](egbert.md) — Wolf folk concealed as something lesser; Hank's mate; night-worker and winter hunter.
- [Corné Dunham](corne_dunham.md) — badger local guide; knows the valley.
- [Ralph Overhill](ralph_overhill.md) — young badger; loves horses; potential camp follower.

## The Lake of Tears and the towers

- [The Pot-Person](pot_person.md) — small iron puppet in the buried tower; Session 3's heart.
- [The Hermit Shaman](hermit_shaman.md) — reclusive inhabitant of one of the other towers; Sessions 5+.
- [The Storm Mage](storm_mage.md) — original owner of the Lightning Tower; still alive in the hills nearby; age unknown, mind broken. The Lightning Tower's lever.

## The wilds

- [Tod](tod.md) — the forbidden woods' chaos spirit wearing a dead fox boy's body; named itself "Tod" (*fox*) after the creature it wears. The party's gentle introduction to the chaos-spirit ecology.

## Wanderers, drifters, survivors of the last war

- [Brother Darrel](brother_darrel.md) — Beaver folk ex-sergeant; rejects money; brought Hank and Egbert to Ingram's orphanage in Salmonswell.
- [Ingram](ingram.md) — very old male Mouse; keeper of the Salmonswell orphanage; held Darrel's name and wages for decades.
- [Eric Randell](eric_randell.md) — fox animal wrangler with a transport Dromo bird, Gato-walker.
- [Nadine](nadine.md) — Finch trader from Raft city; face of a travelling merchant group; brings news of the larger world when the campaign needs it.

## World Edge

- [Master Darius](master_darius.md) — badger forge-master.
- [Remi](remi.md) — rat factory worker, expelled from World Edge with his kin; carries Darius's letter of recommendation.
