# Narissa

- **Role:** The First Mage; historical/legendary figure
- **Species:** Human
- **Location:** First settlements; encountered her defining event on a lake
- **Status:** Deceased (long-lived, into her hundreds)

## Summary

A 24-year-old colonist who became the first confirmed mage after an involuntary fusion with a water/earth spirit. Pulled underwater by a whirlpool, she emerged bound to the spirit, which remained with her for the rest of her life and extended her lifespan far beyond the human norm. She suffered constant nightmares — not her own fears but the formless restlessness of an ancient spirit unable to adapt to human sleep. Her diary, recording her fusion and what she learned from it, became the foundational text of all magical knowledge that followed and sparked the Age of Wild Magic as others tried (mostly catastrophically) to replicate her binding. After her death the spirit lingered in her cloak, which was said to move on its own and leave water trails across dry ground.

## Relationships

- None named directly. Her diary became the indirect teacher of every mage that followed.
