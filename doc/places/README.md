# Places

Settings and locations in the Moros setting. Parallel structure to `doc/npcs/`.

## Reference

- [Relative Geography](geography.md) — constraint list of relative positions; the foundation for the future actual map.
- [Location Cards](cards.md) — quick-reference cards for each major place (DM rumour-reference + player-earnable standing).

## Settlements

- [Salmonswell](salmonswell.md) — independent small city well up-river; salmon runs at the Upwelling.
- [Fata Morgana](fata_morgana.md) — big city inside the southern desert; unique (but limited) protection against chaos spirits.
- [Linar Harbour](linar_harbour.md) — small independent east-coast harbour; start of the main trade road; most of its working population is descended from overseas settlers who stayed after the war.

## Regions and hazards

- [The Forbidden Woods](forbidden_woods.md) — a forest that holds a chaos spirit; warped trees; barrier to Raft city.
- [Haven](haven.md) — iron-puppet settlement beside the shifting lands to the west; hard to reach unless actively sought.

## Canonical registry

RULES.md §"World: Places of Origin" remains the master registry of valid place names. Entries here add detail for places that have earned their own page; anything that is still just a line in RULES.md does not yet need a file.
