# Moros — Statistics

Every character has 8 statistics, each starting at **Level 1**. Statistics grow through
progression choices. Each statistic has a named action and unlocks further abilities at higher
levels.

---

### Charisma (Char) — Inspire

The force of personality that moves people. A high-Charisma character leads, persuades, and
holds a group together under pressure — not merely through charm, but through the conviction
that makes others listen and believe.

| Scenario | Use |
|---|---|
| combat | Redirect allies, break enemy morale without raising a weapon |
| stealth | Bluff or talk your way past a guard |
| negotiation | Persuade, perform, command the room |
| exploration | Rally the group through hardship and uncertainty |
| travel | Keep spirits high over long miles |
| camp | Mindless, meticulous work empties the mind *(defocus)* |
| market | Gather rumours, build relationships, open doors |
| forage | Lift the group's spirits while searching |

**Actions:**
- **strategy** *(Char ≥ 3)* — All players may add another card to the pile; remove one card per
  Char above 3.
- **boost** *(Char ≥ 4)* — Players may play two cards next round instead of one.
- **rallying cry** *(Char ≥ 5)* — Every ally recalls one card from their discard.
  Tension rises by 1.

---

### Dexterity (Dex) — Action

Precision of body — the discipline that places force exactly where it needs to go. Throwing,
catching, climbing, sneaking, riding: Dexterity governs every action that demands fine control
over raw strength.

| Scenario | Use |
|---|---|
| combat | Block incoming blows, deflect thrown objects, land accurate strikes |
| stealth | Slip past guards without a sound |
| negotiation | Juggle to impress, or vanish from sight when things go wrong |
| exploration | Climb walls, squeeze through tight gaps |
| travel | Stay in the saddle when the terrain fights back |
| camp | A quiet nap — the body goes still and the reflexes reset *(defocus)* |
| market | Sleight of hand, pickpocket |
| forage | Pick plants quietly near predators |

**Actions:**
- **feint** *(Dex ≥ 3)* — Swap your played card with another from your hand after your
  opponent commits.
- **flourish** *(Dex ≥ 4)* — Force the enemy to waste their next action on you.

**Specializations:** Shield, Parry, Blocking, Sneak, Juggle, Climbing

---

### Endurance (Endu) — Resist

The body's willingness to keep going. Every blow taken, every sleepless night, every league
beyond exhaustion — Endurance is the wall between a character and collapse. It sets how much
they can carry and how much punishment they can absorb.

| Scenario | Use |
|---|---|
| combat | Absorb damage that would drop a lesser character; measures how long you keep standing |
| stealth | Hold a position motionless for as long as it takes |
| negotiation | Loom with physical presence and gravity |
| exploration | Brave harsh conditions inside without faltering |
| travel | Set the group's pace without complaint; determines carrying capacity |
| camp | Sleep deeply and completely — full rest clears the strain *(defocus)* |
| market | Stand long hours, carry heavy loads |
| forage | Endure cold, hunger, and wet while searching |

**Actions:**
- **dig in** *(Endu ≥ 3)* — Ignore the next source of damage or complication entirely.
  Cannot be used two rounds in a row.
- **second wind** *(Endu ≥ 4)* — Discard your hand and draw three fresh cards; any Endu shown
  on them may be added to your next action.

---

### Handiness (Hand) — Trick

The practical intelligence of the hands. Crafting, repairing, disarming, sleight of hand —
Handiness turns materials into solutions. A handy character is never truly without tools, because
they can make something useful out of almost anything.

| Scenario | Use |
|---|---|
| combat | Strip a weapon from an opponent's grip with technique, not force |
| stealth | Pick a lock or disable a trap without making a sound |
| negotiation | Produce the right item at exactly the right moment |
| exploration | Improvise tools on the spot, disarm traps |
| travel | Keep equipment functional; a well-maintained kit prevents disasters on the road |
| camp | Repair gear, cook a meal — hands reconnect focus to purpose *(refocus)* |
| market | Appraise goods accurately, barter shrewdly |
| forage | Identify edible plants, fashion snares |

**Actions:**
- **disarm** *(Hand > Dex + Might)* — Send the enemy's weapon flying; they can retrieve it in a
  turn.
- **improvise tool** *(Hand ≥ 4)* — Create a temporary item from nearby scraps; it acts as a
  matching item for one scene then is used up.

**Specializations:** Woodworking, Cloth, Leather, Smithing, Healer, Machinist, Jeweler, Cooking

---

### Might — Battle

The raw authority of physical force. Close combat, breaking things down, hauling heavy loads, and
radiating a presence that makes threats credible — Might is not simply strength, it is the body's
capacity to impose its will on the world.

| Scenario | Use |
|---|---|
| combat | Strike hard in close quarters; every swing carries full weight |
| stealth | Restrain or silence a guard before the alarm is raised |
| negotiation | Loom and threaten; let the body's power speak before the mouth does |
| exploration | Force doors open, shift rubble and obstacles |
| travel | Shoulder heavy loads and keep moving |
| camp | Chop wood, build shelter; a calm walk lets the muscles unwind *(defocus)* |
| market | Carry merchandise, deter bad actors |
| forage | Clear obstacles to reach food sources |

**Actions:**
- **power through** *(Might ≥ 3)* — Add Might to the total even when the card played has no
  Might symbol.
- **cleave** *(Might ≥ 5)* — The attack applies to every enemy in reach; each target beyond
  the first reduces the total by 2.

**Specializations:** Axes, Blunt, Brawl, Pole weapon, Spear, Swords

---

### Perception (Perc) — Spot

The gift of noticing — and the discipline of using what you notice. Ranged attacks, detecting
deception, reading a scene before it turns hostile, tracking quarry: Perception turns raw
information into decisive advantage before others realize something is there.

| Scenario | Use |
|---|---|
| combat | Land ranged attacks with precision, read the field to find weaknesses |
| stealth | Spot guard patterns and safe windows of movement |
| negotiation | Detect lies, read intent, notice what people are trying hard not to show |
| exploration | Find hidden passages, concealed items, and overlooked clues |
| travel | Scout ahead, read the terrain, warn the group before trouble arrives |
| camp | Stand watch, detect threats before they reach the camp *(refocus)* |
| market | Spot a deal, catch a cheat, see what others miss |
| forage | Track animals, identify edible plants and safe water |

**Actions:**
- **called shot** *(Perc ≥ 4)* — Declare a specific effect before rolling (disarm, trip, blind);
  success delivers that effect on top of normal damage.
- **weak point** *(Perc > Dex + Might)* — Expose a gap in the enemy's defenses; an ally may
  play a second card on their next attack against this target.

**Specializations:** Bows, Crossbow, Sling, Darts, Scouting, Tracking, Navigation

---

### Speed — Move

The freedom to be somewhere else. Dodging, sprinting, closing distance or opening it: Speed
determines who acts first, who escapes, and who arrives when it still matters.

| Scenario | Use |
|---|---|
| combat | Dodge attacks, control space; be where the blow was, not where it lands |
| stealth | Dart between shadows; burst to a new position while staying unseen |
| negotiation | Exit before the conversation turns dangerous |
| exploration | Cover ground quickly; reach areas before hazards can react |
| travel | Set a fast pace and arrive before circumstances can change |
| camp | Hang around without purpose — the body recovers from all that motion *(defocus)* |
| market | Slip through a crowd, cross a room unnoticed |
| forage | Cover more ground in less time; chase down prey |

**Actions:**
- **sprint** *(Speed ≥ 3)* — Move to any position in the scene without spending an action,
  once per round.
- **dodge** *(Reaction, Speed > Dex)* — Step aside from an incoming blow.
- **blur** *(Speed ≥ 5)* — Attack and immediately retreat; the enemy may not counter-attack
  this turn.

---

### Willpower (Will) — Focus

The architecture of intent — the stat that keeps a character themselves when everything tries to
unmake them. Resisting fear, sustaining a power's focus, aiming under chaos: Willpower is the
foundation the other statistics rest on when circumstances turn hostile.

| Scenario | Use |
|---|---|
| combat | Resist fear effects, hold nerve, aim with precision under chaos |
| stealth | Stay completely still and calm under extreme pressure |
| negotiation | See through influence and manipulation; a strong will is the hardest wall to climb |
| exploration | Hold concentration on puzzles and mechanisms despite distraction |
| travel | Keep a clear head over long journeys; mental clarity prevents costly mistakes |
| camp | Lose yourself in a book *(defocus)*; work through a puzzle to sharpen focus *(refocus)* |
| market | Resist pressure sales and con artists |
| forage | Stay focused and purposeful despite hunger and exhaustion |

**Actions:**
- **steel the mind** *(Will ≥ 3)* — Negate a fear, charm, or confusion effect on yourself or
  an adjacent ally.
- **lock on** *(Will ≥ 4)* — Choose one target; all your cards count as having that target's
  weakness for the rest of the scene.

**Specializations:** Religion, Shamanic, Magic, Druid, Monk
