# Moros — Powers

Each racial power can be applied across scenario types wherever its description or statistics
give a meaningful advantage. Powers are race-specific — a character may only learn powers from
their own race. Learning a power raises one of its two linked statistics by 1 and generates a
card for play. There are 37 powers in total.

**Powers apply to creatures, not only to scenarios.** A power's description does not list every
creature it can be used on; the working principle is *if the creature's nature makes the power
applicable, the power applies*. *Druid* on accommodated fauna and flora; *Shaman* on spirits
and chaos creatures; *Hunter* tracking a beast; *Smell* on contamination; *Lookout* on
something in ambush; the right command-phrase stopping a bound construct. See `CREATURES.md`
§"Powers on creatures" for the cross-reference.

See [RULES.md — Races and Powers](RULES.md#races-and-powers) for which races have access to
which powers.

---

### Adaptation *(Will + Char — shadow step)*
*Move around increasingly undetected.*
- **Stealth** — primary use; move to any shadow or cover in the scene as a free action once
  per round; detection checks targeting this character in stealth gain −1
- **Combat** — flank enemies without triggering a reaction; may reenter stealth after being
  spotted once per scene without spending an extra action
- **Exploration** — slip past an unaware guardian or environmental hazard without a Dex check;
  if a guardian is aware, the Perc check to detect this character gains −1
- **Market** — move through a crowd without a Speed or Dex check; cannot be tailed unless the
  pursuer's Perc exceeds this character's Will

- **Overwhelmed** — You forget which face is yours. Masks accumulate until no original remains.

### Balance *(Dex + Will — tumble)*
*Moving erratically; eventually impossible to hit.*
- **Combat** — primary use; erratic movement makes you a hard target; attackers targeting this
  character with cutting, impaling, or blunt damage require Might or Dex 1 higher than their stat
  to land a hit
- **Stealth** — unpredictable movement imposes −1 on Perc checks to track this character;
  a pursuer must exceed this character's Speed to maintain pursuit
- **Exploration** — environmental hazards targeting specific positions (falling debris,
  unstable ground, collapsing floors) do not affect this character; no Dex check required

- **Overwhelmed** — The motion never stops. Your body has forgotten how to be still.

### Blood *(Will + Might — regenerate)*
*Borrow the life energy from someone else.*
- **Combat** — drain a nearby enemy or restore an ally's ability to keep fighting; deals draining
  damage (target Will −1); alternatively, redirect the drain to restore one lost stat point to an
  ally
- **Camp** — draw on a small life source (a plant, an insect, a willing donor) to restore 1
  stat point to yourself or one ally without spending a short rest action
- **Forage** — draw ambient life energy from rich natural terrain (forest, river, meadow) to
  sustain yourself without food for one scenario; arid or dead terrain cannot provide this
- **Negotiation** — the drain creates a disquieting presence; while Blood is active Tension
  cannot rise above its starting level for the scene; a target who lies visibly loses composure
  — the DM must reveal the tell

- **Overwhelmed** — Hunger for life energy overrides all else. You reach without deciding to.

### Camp *(Char + Endu — shielding)*
*Find a safe place to recuperate.*
- **Camp** — primary use; short rest restores 1 additional stat point for all characters this
  character tends; the group cannot be surprised during camp while Camp is active
- **Exploration** — locate a defensible position in any ruin within one scene without a Perc
  check; allies sheltering there gain +1 to the first action of any combat that begins there
- **Travel** — identify a rest point before Endu penalties from sustained travel apply; negates
  1 Endu penalty for the group on the round it would first trigger
- **Forage** — locate a safe clearing automatically; allies who take a short rest there restore
  1 additional stat point

- **Overwhelmed** — You cannot leave. The safe place must be made safer still. The group waits.

### Charge *(Speed + Might — prone)*
*Increasingly impossible to be stopped.*
- **Combat** — primary use; crash through enemy lines and knock targets prone; deals blunt damage
  (target Might −1); targets with Speed < 4 are knocked prone and lose their next action
- **Travel** — physical barriers (fallen trees, boulders, flooding) do not slow the group;
  the Charge character removes the obstacle and the group passes at full Speed
- **Exploration** — break through a reinforced door, wall section, or barricade in one
  action; Might always adds to the total for this action regardless of the card drawn

- **Overwhelmed** — Once moving, you cannot stop. Everything in your path becomes an obstacle.

### Clan *(Char + Will — shelter)*
*Ensures aid when encountering kindred people.*
- **Market** — primary use; once per scenario find a member of the same race who provides one
  piece of local information or a safe refuge without a Char check; invoking clan ties reduces
  Tension by 1
- **Negotiation** — invoke clan ties to find common ground; if Will or Char exceeds the target's,
  Tension drops by 1 and the target cannot raise Tension on their next action
- **Camp** — kin encountered in camp provide shelter, food for one character, or one piece of
  local information; no Char check required; when the whole group camps together the bond of
  chosen kin is enough — all resting characters restore 1 additional stat point from short rest
- **Travel** — encounters with kin in their own territory do not raise Tension; hostile NPCs of
  the same race require a Char check before they will attack
- **Combat** — when an ally falls Broken, this character moves immediately to their side as a
  free action and stabilises them without a Hand check; the clan does not leave its own behind;
  once per scene

- **Overwhelmed** — Every outsider reads as threat. Beyond your kin, the world is enemy ground.

### Claw *(Might + Dex — sneak)*
*Increased damage when attacking unnoticed.*
- **Combat** — primary use; strike from concealment for increased damage; deals cutting damage
  (target Dex −1); cutting mitigation from armor does not apply when striking an unaware target
- **Stealth** — bring down a single unaware target silently; the takedown triggers no
  detection check provided no other guard is within Perc range of the target
- **Forage** — hunt by approaching undetected; a successful undetected approach counts as a
  guaranteed snare result (food for one character) without a further Perc action

- **Overwhelmed** — You strike before you decide to. The unaware draw your claw like a reflex.

### Climber *(Dex + Might — reach)*
*Scale walls and trees.*
- **Exploration** — primary use; vertical surfaces require no Dex check to scale; can carry
  one additional person while climbing without Speed penalty
- **Stealth** — scale to a vantage point; detection checks from below gain −1; attacking from
  the height costs one action to descend afterward
- **Travel** — rocky or vertical terrain does not reduce Speed; the group may follow this
  character through vertical terrain without a Dex check
- **Combat** — attacks from an elevated position gain +1; enemies below cannot use reach
  weapons against this character while they hold the height
- **Forage** — reach fruit, nests, or prey in trees

- **Overwhelmed** — The ground becomes unbearable. You ascend everything and refuse to come down.

### Control *(Endu + Perc — shape)*
*Modify your form; possibly stronger and more enduring.*
- **Combat** — redistribute mass to meet the moment; raise one stat by +1 and lower another by
  −1; the shift is visible up close, lasts as long as Control is active, and can be reallocated
  once per scene as the body reshapes itself to the next demand
- **Stealth** — compress the silhouette; Dex-based stealth actions gain +1
- **Exploration** — compress to fit through a gap one size smaller than normal; no Dex check
  required for the passage
- **Travel** — reshape posture and surface area against wind and rain; Speed and Endu penalties
  from severe weather do not apply to this character
- **Camp** — thicken skin and redistribute warmth inward; cold and wet camp penalties do not
  apply; this character rests as if sheltered regardless of conditions

- **Overwhelmed** — Form shifts without intent. The body refuses to settle; others cannot read you.

### Digging *(Endu + Might — breaching)*
*Dig through the earth at an increasing speed.*
- **Exploration** — dig through cave-ins and sealed chambers; clears one action's worth of
  loose earth or rubble per round; hard stone takes two rounds per action
- **Stealth** — tunnel under a barrier at one body-length per action; the tunnel is passable by
  one person at a time and makes no sound beyond Perc range
- **Camp** — dig a sheltered hollow in one camp action; provides tent-equivalent shelter; enemies
  approaching underground are detected automatically
- **Forage** — forage yields 1 additional character's food when underground sources are available;
  no Perc check required to locate buried food in suitable terrain

- **Overwhelmed** — Open sky becomes threatening. You tunnel toward darkness and refuse to surface.

### Druid *(Will + Might — nature)*
*Let nature come to your aid.*
- **Forage** — primary use; forage produces food for one additional character without an extra
  round; edible plants and safe water within reach are identified without a Perc check
- **Combat** — call on the natural world to fight alongside you; damage type and stat reduced
  match the summoned creature's primary attack; see CREATURES.md for specifics
- **Exploration** — natural hazards (vines, roots, unstable earth) do not trigger against
  this character; one natural clue per scene is identified without a Perc check
- **Travel** — difficult terrain (dense growth, bog, rockfall) does not reduce the group's
  Speed; Endu penalties from sustained travel are reduced by 1

- **Overwhelmed** — Nature reclaims you. Words come slowly; roots reach. People grow distant.

### Flight *(Speed + Dex — flying)*
*At first a glide; eventually fully free flight.*
- **Exploration** — at mastery 1, glide only: fall any height without damage, no altitude
  gain; at mastery 2 or higher, full flight: move to any position in the scene without
  spending an action
- **Travel** — cover ground by air; at mastery 2 or higher travel distance doubles compared
  to the group's ground pace
- **Stealth** — approach from above; detection checks against this character while airborne
  gain −1 per mastery level
- **Combat** — attacks against grounded opponents from the air gain +1; grounded enemies cannot
  use reach weapons against this character while airborne
- **Forage** — survey the area from height; forage Perc action gains +1; at mastery 2 or higher,
  the full area is surveyed in one action without needing to move through it

- **Overwhelmed** — You refuse to land. The ground is a trap. You circle above, unreachable.

### Fur *(Endu + Dex — defense)*
*Increased protection from harm including the elements.*
- **Combat** — absorb physical damage; reduces incoming blunt damage by 1 (minimum 0); also
  reduces fire and cold damage by 1 each
- **Travel** — cold, rain, and wind weather penalties do not apply; Speed and Endu penalties from
  severe weather are negated
- **Camp** — sleep in exposed conditions without a shelter penalty; full rest restores 1
  additional stat point compared to unsheltered rest
- **Forage** — push through thorns, brambles, and cold water without slowing

- **Overwhelmed** — Feeling leaves you. Pain, warmth, touch — all muffled. The world recedes.

### Hearing *(Perc + Char — information)*
*Increased sensitivity to sound.*
- **Stealth** — detect approaching guards one full round before they arrive; the group cannot
  be surprised while Hearing is active
- **Exploration** — Perc checks to detect hidden mechanisms, distant movement, or hollow
  walls gain +1; concealed exits are automatically noticed within reach
- **Negotiation** — overhear private conversations without a Perc check if the conversation
  is in the same building
- **Market** — pick up gossip and rumours from across the room without a Perc check
- **Camp** — hear approaching threats one full round before they arrive; provides
  Lookout-equivalent watch without spending a separate action

- **Overwhelmed** — Every sound arrives at full volume. You cannot pick one thread from the rest.

### Hide *(Endu + Will — armor)*
*Protection from cutting and stabbing.*
- **Combat** — primary use; reduce damage from blades and points; reduces incoming cutting and
  impaling damage by 1 each (minimum 0)
- **Travel** — environmental damage from scrapes, thorns, and debris does not apply; Speed
  penalties from dense terrain are negated
- **Forage** — push through dense undergrowth without harm or Speed penalty; forage in dense
  terrain requires no Endu check

- **Overwhelmed** — The hide thickens past your choosing. You stop flinching; you stop feeling.

### Hunter *(Perc + Hand — catching)*
*Tracking and snaring prey.*
- **Forage** — primary use; forage produces food for one additional character without an extra
  round; passive snares set during a Gather action yield results at the start of the next scene
- **Exploration** — track creatures or people without a Perc check; identifies creature type,
  size, and recency of trail automatically
- **Stealth** — apply predator-avoidance to detection; Perc checks targeting this character
  while moving through natural terrain gain −1; at mastery 1, cannot be tracked by Smell
- **Travel** — set passive snares during travel; they yield food for one character at the start
  of the next scene without spending a camp action

- **Overwhelmed** — Everyone becomes prey. You track allies without realising it.

### Ingenuity *(Hand + Will — improvise)*
*The uncanny ability to create anything from scraps.*
- **Exploration** — improvise a tool or simple weapon; the item carries Hand + one relevant
  stat (Might for weapons, Dex for tools) and lasts the full scenario with no material check
- **Camp** — build shelter or utility structures from available materials; provides
  tent-equivalent shelter for the group without carrying a tent
- **Forage** — fashion a trap from gathered materials; the trap yields food for one character at
  the start of the next scene without a further Perc action
- **Combat** — improvise a weapon mid-scene; it carries Hand only and is consumed after one use;
  no material check required

- **Overwhelmed** — Nothing can be left as it is. Every mechanism, lock, and wall must be remade.

### Jaws *(Might + Endu — grab)*
*Increased damage from biting; grab and hold.*
- **Combat** — primary use; bite for extra damage and lock an enemy in place; deals cutting damage
  (target Dex −1) and immediately applies grab (target Speed −1 while held); while held, the
  target cannot use two-handed weapons
- **Stealth** — seize a single unaware target silently; the target is grabbed (Speed −1) and
  cannot call out; the takedown triggers no detection check if no other guard is within Perc
  range; a held target can be questioned — counts as one free Negotiation action against them
  while the grip holds
- **Forage** — crack open sources other characters cannot access — shellfish, marrow bones,
  frozen carcasses, nuts in the shell; forage in terrain requiring hard access gains +1 and
  yields food for one additional character from these sources
- **Exploration** — wrench open sealed doors, rusted locks, or jammed hatches in one action
  without a Hand check; grip a surface against a sweep, current, or collapse — this character
  cannot be moved involuntarily while holding

- **Overwhelmed** — You cannot let go. Once locked, the grip holds past sense or safety.

### Labyrinth *(Perc + Will — navigate)*
*Navigate and create mazes.*
- **Exploration** — primary use; navigation through complex spaces requires no Perc check;
  leading a pursuer into a known layout makes their navigation fail without a Speed contest
- **Stealth** — lead a pursuing enemy into a dead end; pursuit into a known space
  automatically fails unless the enemy's Speed is ≥ 2 above this character's
- **Travel** — confusing or shifting terrain (illusion mazes, fog, unstable layouts) imposes no
  navigation penalty; the group cannot be misdirected by environmental confusion
- **Market** — navigating any settlement requires no Perc check; reach any district in the
  minimum number of actions without asking for directions

- **Overwhelmed** — You start building mazes without realising. You stop wanting to be found.

### Lookout *(Perc + Endu — guarding)*
*Notice the tiniest details.*
- **Stealth** — identify guard patrol timing and safe windows one round before acting; the
  group gains +1 to the first stealth action of the scene
- **Exploration** — Perc checks to find concealed doors, hidden items, and clues gain +1;
  at mastery 2, a concealed passage is automatically noticed within one scene
- **Camp** — the group cannot be surprised during camp while Lookout is active; watch
  requires no Perc check and detects threats before they enter the campsite
- **Travel** — detect danger ahead before it becomes a problem; the group cannot be
  surprised on the road while Lookout is active

- **Overwhelmed** — Every shadow hides a threat. Every pause is betrayal. You cannot rest.

### Magic *(Will + Hand — elemental)*
*A spirit was bound to you at initiation. Its elemental nature is yours to channel — and it has
never stopped pushing back.*

At character creation, choose the spirit's type:
- **Broad spirit** — carries 2–4 elements (chosen at creation); the mage may use any of them
- **Focused spirit** — carries one element (chosen at creation); all Magic action totals using
  that element gain +1; the Overwhelmed draw is interpreted with added intensity

- **Combat** — direct elemental force against enemies; damage type and stat reduced depend on the
  element used: Flame → fire/Endu −1; Water → cold/Speed −1; Wind → electric/Perc −1;
  Earth → blunt/Might −1; Iron → impaling/Endu −1; Light → radiant/Will −1;
  Dark → draining/Will −1; Plants → grab/Speed −1
- **Exploration** — one element per action: shift a physical obstacle, create torch-equivalent
  light, or seal or unseal a passage; each use costs the character's commit for the round
- **Stealth** — Dark suppresses all light in one area for one scene; Wind imposes −1 on
  detection checks targeting the group; Plants creates concealment equivalent to dense cover
- **Forage** — Plants doubles forage yield for one scene; Fire prepares food without a camp
  stop, granting the Cooking quality bonus; Water locates the nearest water source without a
  Perc check

- **Overwhelmed** — The spirit finds the gap and takes it. What happens next is its will, not
  yours — shaped by its element and its grievance at the arrangement.

### Musical *(Char + Dex — atmosphere)*
*Influence the mood when people pay attention.*
- **Negotiation** — after a successful Char action, Tension drops by 1 at the end of that
  scene; a targeted performance forces one contact to reveal their reaction before concealing it
- **Market** — drawing a crowd opens contacts that would otherwise require Char ≥ 3; Tension
  cannot rise above its starting level while the performance continues
- **Camp** — reduces the round count for short rest by 1; Relaxed's recovery bonus stacks
  with Musical's presence if both are active
- **Travel** — set a marching rhythm; the group moves in time and the road feels shorter;
  Endu penalties from sustained travel are delayed by 1 round for all characters who can hear
  the performance
- **Combat** — a rallying phrase or sharp beat lifts one ally at the moment they need it;
  that ally gains +1 to their next action; no action required if the Musical character has
  already committed a card this round

- **Overwhelmed** — You cannot stop performing. Every interaction is a stage; you cannot be honest.

### Night *(Perc + Dex — stalk)*
*See clearly in the dark; blend with shadows.*
- **Stealth** — primary use; darkness imposes no Perc penalty; Stealth actions in darkness
  gain +1; targets without Night fighting in darkness take −1 to their actions; darkness
  includes deep forest canopy, overcast nights, underground passages, heavy fog, and the
  interior of unlit structures
- **Exploration** — navigate unlit spaces at full effectiveness without a torch or Perc
  penalty; the darkness definition above applies here too
- **Combat** — the first offensive action from darkness each scene gains +1; entering combat
  from a dark position does not automatically reveal the character's location
- **Forage** — while allies sleep, this character moves through camp and the surrounding area
  as a free action; take one forage action during any camp scene without sacrificing rest;
  nocturnal creatures and plants available only at night are identified and gathered without
  a Perc check

- **Overwhelmed** — Daylight becomes pain. You retreat into shadow. The dark is the only comfort.

### Nimble *(Speed + Dex — dash)*
*Get a burst of speed moving on all fours.*
- **Combat** — reposition to any adjacent cover without spending an action, once per round;
  Dex-based actions taken immediately after repositioning gain +1
- **Stealth** — dash silently between cover without a Speed check; the movement does not
  break concealment
- **Exploration** — cross standard obstacles (gaps, debris, low ceilings) without spending
  an extra action; no Dex check required
- **Travel** — on open, broken, or uneven terrain this character's Speed counts as 1 higher for
  pace calculation; the group does not slow for this character's movement style
- **Forage** — cover more ground during forage; forage produces results for one additional
  character without spending an extra round

- **Overwhelmed** — Your body drops to all fours unbidden. The animal inside steps forward.

### Politics *(Char + Perc — common cause)*
*Know how to reach common ground.*
- **Negotiation** — primary use; once per negotiation the DM reveals one true fact about
  the other party's primary interest; when common ground is identified, Tension cannot rise
  for the rest of that scene
- **Market** — identify faction allegiances of contacts automatically; opening price with a
  faction-adjacent contact is reduced by 1
- **Camp** — Tension drops by 1 at the end of any camp scene where no action raised it that
  round; group conflicts cannot escalate to hostile while Politics is actively engaged
- **Travel** — before the group reaches a settlement, stronghold, or claimed territory,
  identify the dominant faction and their current disposition without a Perc check; the group
  cannot walk into a hostile reception without warning

- **Overwhelmed** — Every word is a move. You cannot stop calculating. Genuine feeling has no place.

### Portage *(Endu + Hand — travel)*
*Haul goods with apparent ease.*
- **Travel** — carrying capacity increases by Endu × 1 additional (doubles the stat's
  contribution to bulk); up to 3 bulk may be transferred from any ally's load per round
  without spending an action
- **Market** — transport and shift large quantities of goods; bulk of carried merchandise
  does not count against the character's own capacity
- **Forage** — carry everything found in one trip regardless of bulk; a second forage scene
  is never required to retrieve finds
- **Exploration** — you carry everything, so you always have what is needed; once per scene
  produce a relevant tool, rope, or material from your pack without a Hand or Perc check;
  no separate item is required

- **Overwhelmed** — You pile more on until upright walking fails. You will not put anything down.

### Relaxed *(Endu + Char — unwind)*
*Radiate calm to the people around you.*
- **Camp** — primary use; allies resting nearby restore 1 additional stat point from short
  rest; Tension in camp cannot rise while this character is present and unthreatened
- **Negotiation** — Tension drops by 1 when Relaxed is actively used to de-escalate; a
  calmed target cannot raise Tension on their next action
- **Stealth** — pass as someone who belongs; guards require a Perc check before challenging
  this character if no obvious violation is present
- **Travel** — this character's calm is contagious on the road; Tension cannot rise during
  travel while they lead; hostile NPCs encountered en route require a Char check before
  turning violent

- **Overwhelmed** — Nothing matters enough to act. Calm becomes indifference. The group moves on.

### Religion *(Char + Will — bless)*
*You have learned the church's practice of reaching the great spirits. Contact is brief and never
certain — but when it lands, the blessing it carries is unlike anything a local spirit can offer.*
- **Combat** — bless allies before or during a fight to improve their capability; blessed
  characters reduce incoming radiant and draining damage by 1 each for the scene
- **Camp** — bless the camp; all resting characters restore 1 additional stat point from short
  rest; hostile creatures cannot locate the camp unless they have Smell or Lookout at mastery 2
- **Negotiation** — Will contributes to Char totals where the backing authority is recognised;
  Tension cannot rise on the round the blessing is invoked
- **Forage** — characters with reduced Endu from hunger restore 1 stat point before the forage
  begins; minimum forage yield is 1 character's food even on a failed Perc action

- **Overwhelmed** — Individual will dissolves. You are the vessel; the power does not stop flowing.

### Scolding *(Might + Will — taunt)*
*Grab the attention of enemies.*
- **Combat** — primary use; taunt enemies to control their focus and protect allies; deals no
  direct damage; enemies redirected onto the taunting character cannot switch focus without
  spending an extra action
- **Stealth** — create a distraction; redirected guards move away from the target area for one
  full round per point of Might; they cannot re-enter until they succeed on a Perc check
- **Negotiation** — apply aggressive pressure; if Will exceeds the target's Will, Tension drops
  by 1 and the target reveals their position or intent before they can conceal it

- **Overwhelmed** — You cannot stop provoking. Aggression precedes thought — allies are not exempt.

### Scrounger *(Hand + Perc — provisions)*
*Find useful supplies in unlikely places.*
- **Forage** — primary use; forage always yields at least enough food for one character even
  on a failed Perc action; a second result in the same scene yields materials as well as food
- **Exploration** — produces one usable item from debris at the end of every exploration
  scene without spending an action
- **Camp** — gather fuel, cordage, and materials from the surroundings without a Perc check
- **Market** — one item per market scene is available below its normal price; the item is
  always relevant to the group's current needs

- **Overwhelmed** — You hoard. Nothing leaves your pack. It grows heavy with what others call waste.

### Scurry *(Dex + Perc — sneaking)*
*Burst of speed while staying hard to spot.*
- **Stealth** — primary use; move to any position in the scene while in stealth without
  breaking concealment, once per round; no action cost
- **Combat** — reposition to any position after a card is committed but before the result
  resolves; does not cost an action
- **Exploration** — dart through a hazardous area before it reacts; no Dex check required; the
  hazard cannot trigger a second time against this character in the same round

- **Overwhelmed** — You bolt before deciding to. By the time you stop, you are alone and lost.

### Shamanic *(Will + Endu — binding)*
*Interact with the spirits around you.*
- **Combat** — bind a spirit to impose −1 Will on one target for one round; maintaining
  the effect costs one action per round and the spirit can be transferred to a new target
- **Exploration** — consult local spirits; the DM reveals one true fact about the place without
  a Perc check; hostile spirits can withhold if their Will exceeds this character's
- **Camp** — bound protective spirits provide Lookout-equivalent watch through the night
  without the character being awake; a bound spirit detects any threat before it enters camp
- **Forage** — ask nature spirits where food or water can be found; forage Perc action gains +1;
  on a success, yield covers one additional character
- **Negotiation** — bring a spirit as witness; while present, neither party can knowingly lie —
  a lie attempt forces a Will check (failure raises Tension by 1)

- **Overwhelmed** — Spirits stop leaving. They crowd in until you cannot tell them from yourself.

### Sleeper *(Will + Endu — recover)*
*Recover in almost any circumstance.*
- **Camp** — primary use; full rest restores all stat reductions regardless of weather,
  noise, or shelter quality; weather and interruption penalties to recovery do not apply
- **Travel** — any halt counts as a short rest for this character; restores 1 stat point
  without requiring a full camp scenario
- **Forage** — take a recovery break during a forage scene; restores 1 stat point without
  spending a camp action; the forage round is not lost

- **Overwhelmed** — You drop without warning. Dreams bleed into waking. You act on them as if real.

### Sly *(Char + Hand — trick)*
*Able to trick those around you.*
- **Negotiation** — once per scenario substitute Char for any other stat on one action
  through misdirection; if the action succeeds the deception cannot be revealed that scene
- **Market** — get a better price through clever framing or distraction; Tension cannot rise
  from a Sly action that succeeds
- **Stealth** — create a convincing distraction or false trail that persists for one
  additional scene before being seen through
- **Combat** — the gap created gives one ally +1 to their next action against that target;
  Tension cannot rise from a Sly action that succeeds

- **Overwhelmed** — You lie when truth would serve just as well. You lose track of what is real.

### Smell *(Perc + Endu — tracking)*
*Follow tracks; recognize people by scent.*
- **Forage** — identifies safe food and water without a Hand check; forage Perc actions
  gain +1; poisoned or unsafe sources are detected automatically
- **Stealth** — detect approaching enemies one full round before they arrive; cannot be
  surprised by any creature with a physical scent
- **Exploration** — follow a scent trail without a Perc check; the trail leads directly to the
  target unless it has been deliberately masked
- **Market** — automatically recognise any known contact present in the scene; Perc checks to
  detect something being concealed gain +1
- **Travel** — navigation along a known or scented route requires no Perc check; locate the
  nearest water source automatically within one scene without spending a forage action

- **Overwhelmed** — Scent overwhelms all other sense. You cannot filter it; old instincts take over.

### Swimmer *(Dex + Endu — survival)*
*Open water holds no fear — nor does flood, bog, marsh, heavy rain, or fast mud.*
- **Travel** — all wet terrain (open water, flood, bog, marsh, heavy rain, fast mud) imposes
  no Speed penalty; underwater movement matches full Speed; can dive and hold breath for one
  full scene without an Endu check; flooded ground does not reduce the group's pace while
  this character leads
- **Exploration** — explore flooded passages at full effectiveness; retrieves items from
  submerged locations without extra actions
- **Forage** — fish rivers and lakes or dive for shellfish; forage in or near water gains +1 and
  yields food for one additional character
- **Stealth** — approach via water; no scent trail is left; Smell-based detection cannot track
  this character after a water crossing
- **Combat** — in rain, flood, or any wet terrain, attackers targeting this character from
  outside the water or without Swimmer gain −1; this character's Speed counts as 1 higher
  for all combat actions while fighting in their element

- **Overwhelmed** — Water pulls you off course. You wade in without deciding. Dry land feels wrong.

### Travels *(Speed + Perc — scouting)*
*Become immediately familiar in unknown places.*
- **Travel** — primary use; navigation requires no Perc check; the group cannot become lost
  while this character leads; at the start of any travel scenario, identify one hazard on
  the route before it is reached
- **Exploration** — maps a new location accurately after one scene of moving through it;
  the mental map is permanent
- **Market** — know the layout of a new town without asking
- **Forage** — identify productive foraging areas from prior scouting; forage Perc action gains
  +1; a known productive area yields results without a check

- **Overwhelmed** — You cannot stop moving. Every place reached immediately wants to be left behind.
