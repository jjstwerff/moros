# Moros — Powers

Each racial power can be applied across scenario types wherever its description or statistics
give a meaningful advantage. Powers are race-specific — a character may only learn powers from
their own race. Learning a power raises one of its two linked statistics by 1 and generates a
card for play.

See [RULES.md — Races and Powers](RULES.md#races-and-powers) for which races have access to
which powers.

---

### Adaptation *(Will + Char — shadow step)*
*Move around increasingly undetected.*
- **Stealth** — primary use; step between shadows to pass a monster without being noticed
- **Combat** — flank enemies without triggering a reaction
- **Exploration** — slip past guardians or hazards
- **Market** — move through a crowd without drawing attention

- **Overwhelmed** — You forget which face is yours. Masks accumulate until no original remains.

### Balance *(Dex + Will — tumble)*
*Moving erratically; eventually impossible to hit.*
- **Combat** — primary use; erratic movement makes you a hard target
- **Stealth** — unpredictable movement makes you hard to track
- **Exploration** — tumble past falling debris or unstable ground

- **Overwhelmed** — The motion never stops. Your body has forgotten how to be still.

### Blood *(Will + Might — regenerate)*
*Borrow the life energy from someone else.*
- **Combat** — drain a nearby enemy or restore an ally's ability to keep fighting
- **Camp** — accelerate your own recovery after a brutal day
- **Forage** — sustain yourself by drawing energy from the living world around you

- **Overwhelmed** — Hunger for life energy overrides all else. You reach without deciding to.

### Camp *(Char + Endu — shielding)*
*Find a safe place to recuperate.*
- **Camp** — primary use; find and secure the best rest spot, shield the group from harm
  during the night
- **Exploration** — locate a defensible refuge inside dangerous ruins
- **Travel** — identify a good place to stop before exhaustion sets in
- **Forage** — find a safe clearing to rest during the search

- **Overwhelmed** — You cannot leave. The safe place must be made safer still. The group waits.

### Charge *(Speed + Might — prone)*
*Increasingly impossible to be stopped.*
- **Combat** — primary use; crash through enemy lines and knock targets prone
- **Travel** — power through terrain obstacles without slowing the group
- **Exploration** — break through a barrier blocking the path

- **Overwhelmed** — Once moving, you cannot stop. Everything in your path becomes an obstacle.

### Clan *(Char + Will — shelter)*
*Ensures aid when encountering kindred people.*
- **Market** — primary use; find kin in town who open doors, share information, or offer shelter
- **Negotiation** — invoke clan ties to find common ground
- **Camp** — secure help or hospitality from people of the same kind
- **Travel** — safe passage through territory where your kin are known

- **Overwhelmed** — Every outsider reads as threat. Beyond your kin, the world is enemy ground.

### Claw *(Might + Dex — sneak)*
*Increased damage when attacking unnoticed.*
- **Combat** — primary use; strike from concealment for increased damage
- **Stealth** — silence a guard before raising the alarm
- **Forage** — hunt prey by approaching undetected

- **Overwhelmed** — You strike before you decide to. The unaware draw your claw like a reflex.

### Climber *(Dex + Might — reach)*
*Scale walls and trees.*
- **Exploration** — primary use; climb walls to access areas others cannot reach
- **Stealth** — scale to a vantage point or an escape route above the monster's eyeline
- **Travel** — navigate rocky or vertical terrain
- **Combat** — seize the high ground
- **Forage** — reach fruit, nests, or prey in trees

- **Overwhelmed** — The ground becomes unbearable. You ascend everything and refuse to come down.

### Control *(Endu + Perc — shape)*
*Modify your form; possibly stronger and more enduring.*
- **Combat** — shift form to improve physical capability for the fight
- **Stealth** — alter shape to blend in or reduce profile
- **Exploration** — compress or extend to fit through gaps and openings

- **Overwhelmed** — Form shifts without intent. The body refuses to settle; others cannot read you.

### Digging *(Endu + Might — breaching)*
*Dig through the earth at an increasing speed.*
- **Exploration** — dig through cave-ins, buried passages, or sealed chambers
- **Stealth** — tunnel under a barrier rather than through it
- **Camp** — dig defensive positions or a sheltered hollow
- **Forage** — unearth roots, tubers, and burrowing prey

- **Overwhelmed** — Open sky becomes threatening. You tunnel toward darkness and refuse to surface.

### Druid *(Will + Might — nature)*
*Let nature come to your aid.*
- **Forage** — primary use; nature actively assists in finding food
- **Combat** — call on the natural world to fight alongside you
- **Exploration** — nature smooths the path and reveals the way
- **Travel** — the land cooperates; difficult terrain becomes navigable

- **Overwhelmed** — Nature reclaims you. Words come slowly; roots reach. People grow distant.

### Flight *(Speed + Dex — flying)*
*At first a glide; eventually fully free flight.*
- **Exploration** — fly over obstacles and access elevated areas
- **Travel** — cover ground by air instead of on foot
- **Stealth** — approach from above, silent and unexpected
- **Combat** — aerial advantage over grounded opponents
- **Forage** — survey a wide area from height to spot food sources

- **Overwhelmed** — You refuse to land. The ground is a trap. You circle above, unreachable.

### Fur *(Endu + Dex — defense)*
*Increased protection from harm including the elements.*
- **Combat** — absorb physical damage
- **Travel** — endure cold, wet, or harsh weather without penalty
- **Camp** — sleep comfortably in exposed conditions
- **Forage** — push through thorns, brambles, and cold water without slowing

- **Overwhelmed** — Feeling leaves you. Pain, warmth, touch — all muffled. The world recedes.

### Hearing *(Perc + Char — information)*
*Increased sensitivity to sound.*
- **Stealth** — hear patrols and guards long before they arrive
- **Exploration** — detect hidden mechanisms, distant movement, or hollow walls
- **Negotiation** — overhear private conversations to gain leverage
- **Market** — pick up gossip and rumours from across the room
- **Camp** — hear approaching threats before they reach the camp

- **Overwhelmed** — Every sound arrives at full volume. You cannot pick one thread from the rest.

### Hide *(Endu + Will — armor)*
*Protection from cutting and stabbing.*
- **Combat** — primary use; reduce damage from blades and points
- **Travel** — protection from environmental scrapes, thorns, and debris
- **Forage** — push through dense undergrowth without taking harm

- **Overwhelmed** — The hide thickens past your choosing. You stop flinching; you stop feeling.

### Hunter *(Perc + Hand — catching)*
*Tracking and snaring prey.*
- **Forage** — primary use; track, snare, and catch food efficiently
- **Exploration** — track monsters or people through ruins by their signs
- **Stealth** — understand prey behaviour; apply it to avoiding a predator
- **Travel** — find food along the route without breaking the march

- **Overwhelmed** — Everyone becomes prey. You track allies without realising it.

### Ingenuity *(Hand + Will — improvise)*
*The uncanny ability to create anything from scraps.*
- **Exploration** — improvise tools on the spot to solve obstacles and puzzles
- **Camp** — build shelter from whatever is at hand
- **Forage** — fashion tools for gathering and trapping
- **Combat** — produce an improvised weapon or device when nothing else is available

- **Overwhelmed** — Nothing can be left as it is. Every mechanism, lock, and wall must be remade.

### Jaws *(Might + Endu — grab)*
*Increased damage from biting; grab and hold.*
- **Combat** — primary use; bite for extra damage and lock an enemy in place
- **Stealth** — silently seize and subdue a guard without a weapon

- **Overwhelmed** — You cannot let go. Once locked, the grip holds past sense or safety.

### Labyrinth *(Perc + Will — navigate)*
*Navigate and create mazes.*
- **Exploration** — primary use; move confidently through complex dungeon layouts without
  getting lost
- **Stealth** — lead a pursuing monster into a dead end
- **Travel** — navigate confusing or shifting terrain
- **Market** — move through winding city streets with confidence

- **Overwhelmed** — You start building mazes without realising. You stop wanting to be found.

### Lookout *(Perc + Endu — guarding)*
*Notice the tiniest details.*
- **Stealth** — spot guard patterns and safe windows of movement
- **Exploration** — find concealed doors, hidden items, and overlooked clues
- **Camp** — maintain a reliable watch through the night
- **Travel** — detect danger ahead before it becomes a problem

- **Overwhelmed** — Every shadow hides a threat. Every pause is betrayal. You cannot rest.

### Magic *(Will + Hand — elemental)*
*Bend the existing elements to your will.*
- **Combat** — direct elemental force against enemies
- **Exploration** — use elements to move obstacles, light the way, or bypass barriers
- **Stealth** — suppress light and sound, or use elements to conceal approach
- **Forage** — use elemental power to gather or prepare food

- **Overwhelmed** — Elements act without direction. They answer your mood, not your intent.

### Musical *(Char + Dex — atmosphere)*
*Influence the mood when people pay attention.*
- **Negotiation** — set or shift the emotional tone of the room
- **Market** — draw a crowd, warm up a contact, improve the atmosphere
- **Camp** — raise morale and ease tension with music around the fire

- **Overwhelmed** — You cannot stop performing. Every interaction is a stage; you cannot be honest.

### Night *(Perc + Dex — stalk)*
*See clearly in the dark; blend with shadows.*
- **Stealth** — primary use; see where others are blind and vanish into darkness
- **Exploration** — navigate unlit dungeons or ruins without a light source
- **Combat** — attack from darkness for positional advantage

- **Overwhelmed** — Daylight becomes pain. You retreat into shadow. The dark is the only comfort.

### Nimble *(Speed + Dex — dash)*
*Get a burst of speed moving on all fours.*
- **Combat** — quick repositioning during the fight
- **Stealth** — dash silently between cover
- **Exploration** — rapid movement over and around obstacles
- **Travel** — faster movement on suitable terrain
- **Forage** — chase down prey or cover more ground while searching

- **Overwhelmed** — Your body drops to all fours unbidden. The animal inside steps forward.

### Politics *(Char + Perc — common cause)*
*Know how to reach common ground.*
- **Negotiation** — primary use; find the shared interest that makes agreement possible
- **Market** — navigate town factions and power dynamics
- **Camp** — manage tensions within the group before they become a problem

- **Overwhelmed** — Every word is a move. You cannot stop calculating. Genuine feeling has no place.

### Portage *(Endu + Hand — travel)*
*Haul goods with apparent ease.*
- **Travel** — carry the group's gear without slowing the march
- **Market** — transport and shift large quantities of goods
- **Forage** — carry everything found back to camp without multiple trips

- **Overwhelmed** — You pile more on until upright walking fails. You will not put anything down.

### Relaxed *(Endu + Char — unwind)*
*Radiate calm to the people around you.*
- **Camp** — primary use; help others unwind and recover more fully
- **Negotiation** — de-escalate a tense exchange
- **Stealth** — pass as someone who belongs; calm presence doesn't arouse suspicion

- **Overwhelmed** — Nothing matters enough to act. Calm becomes indifference. The group moves on.

### Religion *(Char + Will — bless)*
*Grant others physical and mental boosts.*
- **Combat** — bless allies before or during a fight to improve their capability
- **Camp** — bless the camp for safe and restorative rest
- **Negotiation** — invoke divine authority to lend weight to an argument
- **Forage** — bless the search; strengthen those weakened by hunger

- **Overwhelmed** — Individual will dissolves. You are the vessel; the power does not stop flowing.

### Scolding *(Might + Will — taunt)*
*Grab the attention of enemies.*
- **Combat** — primary use; taunt enemies to control their focus and protect allies
- **Stealth** — create a distraction that draws guards away from where allies need to go
- **Negotiation** — apply aggressive pressure to force a reaction

- **Overwhelmed** — You cannot stop provoking. Aggression precedes thought — allies are not exempt.

### Scrounger *(Hand + Perc — provisions)*
*Find useful supplies in unlikely places.*
- **Forage** — primary use; find food and supplies where others see nothing
- **Exploration** — uncover useful items among the debris of ruins
- **Camp** — gather fuel, cordage, and materials from the surroundings
- **Market** — find the hidden bargain others overlooked

- **Overwhelmed** — You hoard. Nothing leaves your pack. It grows heavy with what others call waste.

### Scurry *(Dex + Perc — sneaking)*
*Burst of speed while staying hard to spot.*
- **Stealth** — primary use; move quickly between positions without being seen
- **Combat** — reposition in a hurry without exposing yourself
- **Exploration** — dart through a hazardous area before it reacts

- **Overwhelmed** — You bolt before deciding to. By the time you stop, you are alone and lost.

### Shamanic *(Will + Endu — binding)*
*Interact with the spirits around you.*
- **Exploration** — consult local spirits for information about a place
- **Camp** — bind protective spirits to the camp during the night
- **Forage** — ask nature spirits where food or water can be found
- **Negotiation** — bring spiritual entities into an agreement as witnesses or parties

- **Overwhelmed** — Spirits stop leaving. They crowd in until you cannot tell them from yourself.

### Sleeper *(Will + Endu — recover)*
*Recover in almost any circumstance.*
- **Camp** — primary use; sleep soundly in poor conditions and recover fully
- **Travel** — rest while moving; catch brief sleep during a halt
- **Forage** — take a recovery break wherever you are without losing much time

- **Overwhelmed** — You drop without warning. Dreams bleed into waking. You act on them as if real.

### Sly *(Char + Hand — trick)*
*Able to trick those around you.*
- **Negotiation** — misdirect and manipulate to get a better outcome
- **Market** — get a better price through clever framing or distraction
- **Stealth** — create a convincing distraction or false trail
- **Combat** — feint and misdirect to open a gap

- **Overwhelmed** — You lie when truth would serve just as well. You lose track of what is real.

### Smell *(Perc + Endu — tracking)*
*Follow tracks; recognize people by scent.*
- **Forage** — smell out food sources and edible plants
- **Stealth** — detect approaching guards by scent before they arrive
- **Exploration** — follow a scent trail to find what you're looking for
- **Market** — recognise a known contact or detect something being concealed
- **Travel** — track a route or find water by following your nose

- **Overwhelmed** — Scent overwhelms all other sense. You cannot filter it; old instincts take over.

### Swimmer *(Dex + Endu — survival)*
*Open water holds no fear.*
- **Travel** — cross rivers and bodies of water without delay
- **Exploration** — explore flooded passages or underwater chambers
- **Forage** — fish rivers and lakes; dive for shellfish
- **Stealth** — approach a position via water, leaving no trail

- **Overwhelmed** — Water pulls you off course. You wade in without deciding. Dry land feels wrong.

### Travels *(Speed + Perc — scouting)*
*Become immediately familiar in unknown places.*
- **Travel** — primary use; orient quickly and move efficiently through unfamiliar terrain
- **Exploration** — rapidly map and read a new place
- **Market** — know the layout of a new town without asking
- **Forage** — identify productive areas faster than someone unfamiliar with the land

- **Overwhelmed** — You cannot stop moving. Every place reached immediately wants to be left behind.
